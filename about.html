About Bountiful Foods

Bountiful Foods specializies on organic, locally grown foods. At Bountiful Foods, we bring you the finest selection of farm produce straight from the fields, ensuring you experience the true taste of nature in every bite.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags to local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.

Our Vision is to become the forefront providers of fresh fruits drinks and farm produce in the world

History

Bountiful Foods was inspired by the an agricultural ethusiast: Jeremiah Ezekiel, who needed a way to help farmers sell their produce in 1995. Latter in 2005 bountiful foods started producing Healthy Fresh Fruit Drinks of different varities like Banana Shakes, Smoothies and lot more.

Location

Bountiful Foods is located in Carlsbad Califonia.

Our over 40 food trucks can be foud in almost every city fron Los Angeles to San Diego.

Award-winning Products

Over the years, our commitment to quality and excellence has earned us numerous accolades for our products. From prize-winning livestock at regional fairs to the recognition of our organic produce at prestigious food competitions, we take pride in the exceptional taste and nutritional value of everything that comes from our farm. These accolades serve as a testament to our unwavering dedication to delivering the best agricultural products to our customers.
